How this is calculated

  • Strategy NAV at each checkpoint = true_strategy_value (long positions ex-SATA + SATA + cash/pending − short option MTM + long option MTM). This is your actual economic net worth under the flywheel at import time.
  • B&H NAV = (IBIT shares at period start × price at this checkpoint) + (ASST shares at period start × price at this checkpoint) + sidecar value from the start snapshot (SATA + cash + initial option effects, with no premiums added during the period and no short option liability).
  • Positive "Diff" means the flywheel (premiums collected + any other effects) has the account ahead of simply holding those starting shares unoptioned + the starting sidecar.
  • New share purchases or capital added after the chosen start are not included in the B&H line above (choose a later start snapshot after big additions if you want to normalize the comparison).
  • Journal premiums are the net credit/debit on option trades in the date window — the main "extra" generated by the strategy that flows into SATA or is otherwise available.

This is realized, point-in-time tracking from the snapshots you have imported. It complements the forward-looking Scenarios and Monte Carlo pages.
